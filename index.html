About Us

A Canadian firm built to support Canadian businesses

Finitra is a professional services firm offering AI, IT, accounting, and business consulting to small businesses, startups, and individuals across Canada.

We started Finitra because we saw too many small businesses and individuals struggling to get consistent support across technology, finances, and strategy. Our goal is simple: be the one team that covers all of it.

  • ✓
    Practical over theoretical We focus on solutions that work in the real world — not just recommendations that look good on paper.
  • ✓
    One team, multiple disciplines AI, IT, accounting, and strategy under one roof means fewer gaps and better-informed advice across every area.
  • ✓
    Built for Canada We understand CRA rules, provincial regulations, and the Canadian funding landscape — so you don't have to become an expert.
Our Services

What We Offer

Four practice areas, delivered by a team that understands how they connect.

🤖

AI Solutions

We help Canadian businesses move from curiosity about AI to actually using it — identifying where it can save time, reduce costs, and improve decisions, then putting those tools in place.

  • AI readiness assessment & roadmap
  • Workflow automation (repetitive task elimination)
  • AI tool selection & implementation
  • Document processing & data extraction automation
  • AI-assisted reporting & business intelligence
  • Staff training on practical AI use
💻

IT & Technology Consulting

We help businesses and individuals adopt and manage the right technology — from moving to the cloud to securing systems and streamlining daily operations.

  • Cloud setup & migration (Azure, AWS)
  • IT infrastructure planning & support
  • Cybersecurity basics & best practices
  • Software selection & implementation
  • Digital process automation
📊

Accounting & Tax Services

Accurate books and proactive tax planning keep your finances on solid footing — whether you're running a business or managing your personal taxes.

  • Bookkeeping & monthly reporting
  • Corporate tax returns (T2) & personal (T1)
  • HST/GST registration & filing
  • Payroll & CRA remittances
  • Year-end financial statements

Business Consulting & Strategy

From writing a business plan to figuring out your next stage of growth, we work alongside you to clarify direction and put a practical plan in place.

  • Business plan development
  • Market research & competitive analysis
  • Operational process improvement
  • Government grant identification & applications
  • Startup incorporation guidance
